Distribution of Donation Money

As a result of living off of donations, we need a way to distrubute our donation money to necessary uses. The following is our plan for distribution:

  • 50% - Shipping fares of supplies collected
  • 45% - Money donated to other helpful causes (i.e. Recycling4Smiles)
  • 5% - Operational uses (i.e. web hosting, employment, development)

Advertising

Our Course of Advertising is as follows:

  1. Hand out flyers to local communities and neighborhoods
  2. Establish advertisements on Facebook and Google
  3. Get an advertisment on the local newspaper
  4. Attend fundraisers or community events and advertise
  5. Advertise to other schools, cities, and states
  6. Possible branch out to other countries (i.e. Third World)

Employment

We are also allowing opportunities for employment. We offer full-time jobs, part-time jobs, and summer internship programs. We also offer community service hours. Employees receive quite a decent salary each year. The benefits of being an employee are as follows:

  • Complementary Employee Lunches & Dinners (locations may differ)
  • Guaranteed Insurance
  • Ensure Holiday Pay
  • Guaranteed Social Security Disability Insurance
  • 10 Vacation Days
  • Ensure Workers' Compensation

There are also various jobs we offer, such as:

  • Agency Director
  • Supplies Collector
  • Charity Customer Support
  • Tech Support
  • Charity Directors
  • Advertising Agents
